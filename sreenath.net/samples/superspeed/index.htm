Who We Are

We "Super Speed Packers and Movers" a Bangalore Based Company is a widely known service provider offering safe relocation of various household and commercial goods. We are providing an array of services including Packing, Storage, Office shifting, Car transportation, Factory shifting, Transportation, Loading & Unloading as well as Insurance. All our services are handled by the trained staff, who hold extensive experience in the industry.

Incepted in 2004, we have been working under the guidance of Mr. Pavan Singh Rathore – Owner of the company. With the consistent support of Mr. Pavan Singh Rathore, our team professionally manages the packing and moving of all kind of household and industrial goods. Our entire team work in compliance to the customer's requirement and offer them with the tailor made solutions that perfectly meet their needs. We have also developed tie-ups with well knwon transportation companies that assist us in offering hassle-free and timely delivery of assignments anywhere in India.

We pay extra attention to all the related activities like Octroi, sales taxes etc. to make the whole process of moving safe and smooth. Backed by our own fleet of trailers, containers, car-carrier trucks & canters, we ensure safe and secure delivery of client's valuable goods.

Our cost-efficient relocation services, progressive and innovative packing and moving solutions and transparent dealings have earned us a long list of clients in major Indian markets!
