Looking to fast-track your career in technology?

Arizona State University has teamed up with Revature to provide a completely FREE Coding Bootcamp with classes starting in the summer, 2016 at ASU SkySong.


ASU students who successfully complete the Bootcamp will be hired by Revature at a competitive salary to work with Revature clients – including many Fortune 500 firms and leading technology companies.


ASU’s FREE Coding Bootcamp includes:

  • A 12-week immersive, industry aligned training program on technology in-demand by specific Revature clients
  • 12 weeks of accommodation during the Bootcamp
  • All books and training materials needed for the Bootcamp
  • Professional certificates (may include: Microsoft, Oracle, etc.)
  • Dedicated industry mentors to ensure your success during the Bootcamp and first two years of employment
  • ePortfolio to showcase your projects, accomplishments, and certificates
