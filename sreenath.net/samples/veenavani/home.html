Introduction

Sri Veena Vani School of Music has successfully completed 34 years as a prestigious & renowned institution & has done a tremendous achievement by imparting music education to over 4000 students till date, in the age group of 4 to 70 years. About 14 different instruments and vocals in both Carnatic and Hindustani styles, as well as light music and playback singing are taught here

History

Sri Veena Vani School of Music has conducted many stage shows which have been extensively covered by all major newspapers. For four years, the talent-search show, 'Pratibhanveshane' has been attended by about 3000-4000 people. We have also conducted a show where the proceeds have been used to purchase books for the less-fortunate students among us. Our program called Taare Zameen Par, has attracted about 5000 people, and all proceeds in the form of ticket sales have gone to charity. This is apart from the annual stage show conducted by our school which showcases the talents of all our students.

Highlights

This school was started by Vidwan Sri Sampath Kumar Sharma, and was mentored by him for 26 years. He was the student of Mysore Aasthana Vidwan Sri Somashekhar Rajamani.

Sri Gireesh Kumar

Sri Gireesh Kumar, who is the student of Sri Sampath Kumar Sharma, has been nurturing this prominent institution for the past 8 years. He is proficient in 8 instruments and 4 types of vocals. He started his education in music at the age of 6 years & emerged as the sensational winning director of a State-level Drama Competition at the age of 19. He has successfully directed 23 dramas in Kannada and 1 in Hindi. He has also composed splendid music for over 450 stage plays and more than 100 regional language feature films and short films.


Sri Gireesh Kumar has worked as an Assistant Music Director and Sound Engineer under most of the Music Directors of the Kannada Film Industry. Many of the films he has worked on have gone on to become sensational & super-hits, which include

  • Gandu Gali Kumara Rama(Kannada)
  • Anniyan(Tamil) with Harris Jayaraj
  • Gangothri(Telugu) with Kirvani
  • Banni (Telugu) with Devisri Prasad
His other Gurus include
  • Gandu Gali Kumara Rama(Kannada)
  • Anniyan(Tamil) with Harris Jayaraj
